About Me

Learn more about my background, experience and passion for AI & Robotics

Hello, I'm Shon

I am an AI/ML Engineer and Robotics enthusiast with 2.5 years of hands-on experience in developing and deploying end-to-end solutions in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

My expertise lies in creating systems for object detection, classification, and real-time decision-making. I'm proficient in model optimization for embedded and edge devices, with proven expertise in implementing autonomous robotic navigation using SLAM, sensor fusion, and real-time path planning for intelligent and adaptive systems.

Currently working at Humanitarian Technology Labs (Hut Labs), Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, I've been involved in various cutting-edge projects including semi-autonomous systems for the Indian Air Force, autonomous navigation stacks for service robots, and AI-based assistive technologies.

I hold a Master of Computer Applications degree from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham and a Bachelor of Computer Applications from Mahatma Gandhi University.
